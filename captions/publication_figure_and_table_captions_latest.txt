Table 1. Retrospective model selection
--------------------------------------
Comparison of the main retrospective negative binomial count models fitted to the common municipality-year sample. M3 was selected as the parsimonious BIC-supported model for spatial current and future projections, whereas M6 was retained as the AIC-supported full retrospective model. The outcome was probable acute/subacute paracoccidioidomycosis in the 1998-2024 training period.

Table 2. Retrospective prediction performance
---------------------------------------------
Retrospective prediction performance of the selected negative binomial models in the common sample. Performance metrics summarize agreement between observed and model-predicted municipality-year event counts. Because the outcome is rare, rank correlations are expected to be modest and should be interpreted as complementary diagnostics.

Table 3. Predictor group effects
--------------------------------
Summary of predictor group effects in the selected retrospective models. Incidence rate ratios were derived from model coefficients and are summarized by predictor group. The values describe associations in the fitted model and should not be interpreted as isolated causal effects.

Table 4. Current and future suitability summary
-----------------------------------------------
Current and future model-predicted suitability for probable acute/subacute paracoccidioidomycosis. Values are model-predicted annualized suitability rate per 100,000 inhabitants. Future projections replace current BIOCLIM predictors with CMIP6 ensemble predictors and hold soil and elevation constant.

Table 5. Regional future suitability change
-------------------------------------------
Regional summary of future-minus-current suitability change. Values represent the median and mean changes in model-predicted annualized suitability rate per 100,000 inhabitants across municipalities within each region.

Table 6. State-level future suitability change under the most severe scenario
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
State-level summary of future suitability change under SSP585 | 2061-2080. States are ranked by median future-minus-current change in model-predicted annualized suitability rate per 100,000 inhabitants.

Table 7. Municipality-level suitability change categories
---------------------------------------------------------
Distribution of municipalities across future suitability change categories. Categories are based on percent change relative to the current model-predicted baseline. These categories indicate modeled suitability change, not confirmed future incidence.

Table 8. Priority municipalities under the most severe scenario
---------------------------------------------------------------
Priority municipalities under SSP585 | 2061-2080. Municipalities were ranked by a combined score integrating high future predicted suitability and high absolute future-minus-current increase.
